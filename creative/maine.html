Travels: Maine

Date Visited: August '17, '19

Maine is arguably the East Coast state that most encapsulates the vibe of the Northeast (beaches, rocky cliffs, lighthouses, and seafood). My favorite spots include Cape Neddick (where this photo was taken), Ogunquit, Rockport, and Bar Harbor (where Acadia National Park is located). Maine is geared more toward the adventurous soul, as there are many types of activities and terrains to explore. Nevertheless, it is a great place to escape from the city and reconnect with nature (more photos coming soon)..
