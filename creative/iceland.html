Travels: Iceland

Date Visited: August 2017

One of the places that has been on my travel bucklist since I was a child was Iceland. My interest in visiting was sparked again when I stumbled upon Sorelle Amore's Youtube channel, where she gave her tips on visiting the beautiful country. We first landed in Reykjavik, a colorful (and most definitely expensive) city. One of the things that struck me about this city was how clean it was. The water was pure (as expected) and the streets were very clean. Since I only had around 5 days to explore, I wasn't able to do a full circle as I wished to, so I mainly spent my time visiting all the popular attractions, including the Blue Lagoon, the Golden Circle, Þingvellir, Gullfoss, Reynisfjara, and Skógafoss. The landscape was so beautiful and the terrain was something I've never really experienced before (black sand beaches, lagoons, jagged mountaintops). Most notable are the waterfalls, which seemed to encompass all of the landscape. It seemed like everywhere I went, I would always spot one. Iceland is a beautiful country to visit, and definitely a place I would visit again (more photos coming soon)..
