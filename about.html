00. intro

We are taught in school to study hard in order to become successful. Yes, studying is important and I'm very grateful for the education I've received, but this constant repetition of completing homework and taking exams often leads to a static routine that fails to exercise one's creativty. I believe that one's self growth (as important as one's career growth) directly relates to constantly pushing one's creative boundaries. I've learned to appreciate art in its various forms (fashion, music, coding, drawing, writing... the list goes on), and created this outlet to help organize my inspirations & my thoughts. Most importantly, I wanted bring together everything I love into one space. Enjoy!
